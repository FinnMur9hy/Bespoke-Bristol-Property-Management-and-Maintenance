Something not working? Let us know.

Tell us what's wrong, how urgent it is, and when we can get access. We triage every report and get it to a vetted tradesperson as quickly as the urgency needs.

Is this a genuine emergency?

If there's an immediate risk to safety or the property — a strong gas smell, uncontrolled flooding, no working smoke alarm, or no heating in freezing weather — call us now on 0117 123 4567 rather than filling in the form below.

How urgent is it?

Pick the closest match on the form — it helps us prioritise correctly and get the right tradesperson to you.

Emergency

Immediate risk

A risk to safety, health, or serious damage to the property. Call us directly — don't wait for a form response.

e.g. gas smell, uncontrolled leak or flooding, no heating in freezing weather, no working smoke/CO alarm, break-in or security fault.

Urgent

Within 24–48 hours

Affects daily life but isn't dangerous. We'll triage same day and get a tradesperson booked in quickly.

e.g. boiler or hot water fault, leaking pipe, broken external door lock, major appliance failure.

Routine

Can wait

Non-urgent and doesn't affect health, safety or security. We'll schedule it at the next convenient visit.

e.g. dripping tap, sticking door, minor cosmetic damage, squeaky floorboard.
