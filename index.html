Maintenance & Repairs

A tenant reports it, we handle it — you're kept in the loop

Tenants report issues directly to us. We triage every request, use our vetted network of trades, and keep you informed of cost and progress for anything above your agreed spending limit. Routine repairs are handled promptly; anything larger comes to you for approval first.

We charge a flat coordination fee — never a hidden markup on contractor invoices.

  • Vetted plumbers, electricians, gas engineers & handymen
  • Fast triage and response on every report
  • Gas Safety, EICR & EPC renewals tracked proactively
  • Transparent, flat coordination fee — no markups
